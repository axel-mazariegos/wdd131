B•Card Website Plan

Site Name

B•Card – A modern brand representing NFC-powered business cards. The name is short, memorable, and clearly ties to the idea of a digital, smart business card. The logo will be prominently displayed throughout the website.

Site Purpose

The B•Card website introduces visitors to NFC business cards, showcasing their features, benefits, and how to get one. It serves as both an informational hub and a lead generator through direct contact options. The site targets professionals, entrepreneurs, and creatives looking for an innovative way to network.

Scenarios

  • What is an NFC business card, and how does it work?
  • Where can I get a personalized NFC card or contact someone for more information?

Color Schema

  • Lime Green: #C0D731 – Used for buttons, headings, and calls-to-action.
  • Charcoal Black: #010101 – Main text color and background for nav/footer.
  • Off-White: #FFFEFF – Page background.
  • Muted Green (optional): #808F87 – Accent hover or border color.

Typography

  • Poppins: Used for headings, navigation, and bold labels.
  • Roboto: Used for body text and paragraphs.
